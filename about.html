Who We Are

Built for
Tampa Bay.

Garage Door Geeks is a Wesley Chapel-based garage door service company dedicated to the communities of Tampa Bay. We work on residential and commercial garage doors — from emergency spring replacements to full smart opener installations.

We built this company on a simple promise: show up on time, price it fairly, and fix it right. No hidden fees, no unnecessary upsells, no wasted trips. You'll know the full cost before our technician picks up a single tool.

We service all major brands — LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Craftsman, Clopay, Wayne Dalton, CHI, and Amarr — and we carry the parts to get most jobs done in a single visit.
