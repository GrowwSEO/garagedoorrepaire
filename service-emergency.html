What Counts as an Emergency?

Any situation where your garage door failure creates a safety risk, security vulnerability, or operational blockage qualifies as an emergency. You don't need to wait until morning — call us anytime.

Common Emergency Calls We Handle

  • Door stuck open — Your home is unsecured. We treat this as the highest priority.
  • Broken spring at night — Can't get your car in or out. We respond 24/7.
  • Car trapped in garage — Door won't open and you need to leave. Call us.
  • Impact damage — Door was hit by a vehicle and is damaged or off-track.
  • Commercial door blocking operations — Loading bay failure during business hours.
  • Opener failure with no manual override — Door won't open in any mode.

What To Do While You Wait

  • If the door is stuck open: stay inside and keep garage access to your home locked
  • If the door is stuck closed and you need your car: use the manual release cord (red cord hanging from the opener rail) to disengage the opener — then lift manually. Only do this if the door is balanced (no broken spring)
  • Do not try to force a door with a broken spring — it can fall suddenly
  • Don't attempt to re-thread cables or reposition tracks yourself
