Signs of a broken spring include a loud bang from the garage, a door that won't open or feels extremely heavy, visible gaps in the spring coil, or a door that only opens a few inches. If you suspect a broken spring, don't force the door open — call us instead.
Most repairs — spring replacements, cable repairs, opener fixes, and off-track doors — are completed in a single visit of 1 to 2 hours. New door installations typically take 3 to 5 hours depending on door type and complexity.
