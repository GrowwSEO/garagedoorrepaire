Garage Door Repair Services

Garage doors have many moving parts — any one of them can fail and affect the performance or safety of your door. Our technicians arrive with parts stocked for the most common repairs and diagnose the root cause, not just the symptom.

Off-Track Door Repair

An off-track door is one of the most common calls we receive — and one of the most urgent. A door that has jumped off its track is a security risk and should not be forced back into position without professional help, as it can cause further damage or injury.

Common Repair Scenarios

  • Door opens unevenly or tilts to one side
  • Grinding, squeaking, or rattling during operation
  • Door reverses before reaching the ground
  • Door shakes or vibrates excessively
  • Sections binding or catching during movement
  • Bent or damaged panels
  • Bottom seal worn or missing

Our Diagnostic Process

We inspect the full door system on every visit — not just the part you called about. Springs, cables, rollers, hinges, tracks, opener, and safety sensors are all checked. We report what we find, explain the options, and let you decide what to fix. No pressure, no unnecessary add-ons.
