Model 6690 — Premium Pick

The LiftMaster 6690 is our top recommended model for homeowners who want the best. Belt-drive means ultra-quiet operation (ideal if there's a bedroom above the garage), built-in battery backup keeps your door working during power outages, and MyQ Wi-Fi lets you open, close, and monitor your door from your phone.

Model 6580 — Smart Wi-Fi Standard

The 6580 offers all the smart features — MyQ app control, activity alerts, and remote monitoring — in a quiet belt-drive package. A great balance of performance and value for most homes.

Model 2220 — Reliable Value

The chain-drive 2220 is a dependable workhorse for standard residential use. More noise than belt-drive, but excellent reliability and years of trouble-free operation at the most affordable price point.

What Smart Openers Can Do

  • Open and close your door from anywhere via the MyQ app
  • Receive alerts when the door opens or closes
  • Set automatic close schedules
  • Grant temporary access to guests or delivery drivers
  • Integrate with Google Assistant, Alexa, and Apple HomeKey (model dependent)
  • Check door status in real time without being home

Our Installation Process

  • Book online or call — we confirm availability same day
  • Technician removes your old opener and disposes of it
  • New opener installed and rail assembled
  • Remotes and keypad programmed
  • Smart features (Wi-Fi, app) set up and tested with you
  • Full door operation test before we leave

Installation typically takes 1.5 to 2 hours. We don't leave until everything is working perfectly.
