New Door Installation in Tampa Bay

Whether you're replacing a worn-out door, upgrading for curb appeal, or building a new home, Garage Door Geeks handles the full process — supply, delivery, removal of the old door, and installation of the new one.

We work with leading manufacturers and carry doors in all styles, sizes, and materials. Every installation is measured precisely and tested for smooth, balanced operation before we leave.

Door Types We Install

  • Steel Doors — The most popular choice. Durable, low-maintenance, and available in insulated options. Holds up well in Florida's heat and humidity.
  • Steel with Wood Overlay — The look of wood without the maintenance. Carriage-style and traditional designs available.
  • Aluminum Doors — Lightweight, rust-resistant, and great for modern designs. Available with glass panel options.
  • Full View Glass — Modern aluminum frames with tempered glass panels. Popular for contemporary homes and commercial properties.

Brands We Carry

  • Clopay — America's #1 garage door brand. Wide range of styles from traditional raised panel to modern flush.
  • CHI — American-made steel doors with excellent insulation options.
  • Amarr — Quality steel and aluminum doors at a range of price points.
  • Wayne Dalton — Known for their TorqueMaster spring system and wide variety of styles.

Why Replace Your Garage Door?

  • Significant damage, dents, or rust that can't be repaired cost-effectively
  • Outdated style affecting your home's curb appeal or resale value
  • Poor insulation increasing your energy bills
  • Panels that are no longer in production and can't be matched
  • You want smart features or improved security

Our Installation Process

  • Contact us to discuss style, material, and budget
  • We measure your opening precisely and recommend the right door
  • Order placed — most doors arrive within a few days to a week
  • Old door removed and disposed of
  • New door installed, hardware mounted, and springs tensioned
  • Opener (existing or new) connected and tested
  • Full balance and operation test before departure

New door pricing varies by size, material, and style. Contact us for a custom quote — we'll help you find the right door for your home and budget.
